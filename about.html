— The Origin — Our Story

A single desk in 2010. Forty nations today.

Alshendawi was founded in 2010 with a simple but powerful mission: to make international services accessible, transparent, and stress-free for everyone. What began as a small consultancy has grown into a full-service global agency trusted by thousands of individuals and businesses across 40+ countries.

Our founder, driven by personal experience navigating complex immigration and logistics challenges, built Alshendawi to be the partner he never had — one that combines deep expertise with genuine care for every client's outcome.

Today, our team of certified specialists spans immigration law, international logistics, language services, and business consulting — united by a shared commitment to getting results.

— A Letter from our Founder —

On the meaning of passage.

When I first opened the doors of Alshendawi in 2010, I was a young man who had himself navigated the labyrinth of visas, borders, paperwork, and long silences between stamps. I knew the anxiety of waiting. I knew what it felt like to place your future in the hands of a stranger, in a language not your own, for a decision that could not be appealed.

I promised myself — and, in time, every client who walked through our doors — that we would be different. That we would treat each file not as a number in a queue, but as a chapter of a life. That we would answer the telephone with the same care whether the matter was urgent or routine. That we would be honest when a door was closed, and resourceful in finding another.

Fifteen years later, that promise is the only thing that has not changed. Our offices have grown. The nations we serve now number over forty. The languages on our translation desks have multiplied. But the quiet standard — that every passage is personal, every document sacred, every client counseled with discretion — remains our signature.

كل ملفٍ نتلقّاه هو أمانة؛ وكل عميل يأتي إلينا هو فصلٌ من حكاية نشرف بأن نكون جزءًا منها. هذه هي وصيّتنا منذ عام ٢٠١٠، ولن تتغيّر ما بقي في الدار شنداوي.

If you are considering entrusting your passage to us — whether for a visa, a second citizenship, a translated contract, or a new life abroad — I will say only this: we know what this moment means to you. And for as long as we have the privilege of this work, we will honour it.

With warm regard,

Mohamed Nasr Alshendawi

— The House Creed —

Six promises, quietly kept.

Not a list of values. A standard we measure ourselves against, every file, every week.

I.

Honesty before optimism.

If a door is closed, we say so. No fees for hope, no counsel against the odds. The truth, offered early, is the most valuable service we provide.

II.

The file, never a queue.

Every client receives a named counsel, a direct line, and a file handled as if it were the only one on the desk. Because on the day it matters, it is.

III.

Discretion, as a service.

What passes through our doors stays within them. We speak of clients only by their outcomes — and only when permission is given.

IV.

Precision over speed.

A translation, a visa file, a contract — reviewed twice before it leaves our hands. Rushed work is someone else's standard, not ours.

V.

Answered within the hour.

No voicemail labyrinths. No ticket queues. A human reply — in your language — within business hours, every time.

VI.

Local hands, global reach.

Forty-plus nations. Trusted counsel on the ground in each. We never send a file somewhere our people have not already walked.
