Our Story

From a Vision to a Global Presence

Alshendawi was founded in 2010 with a simple but powerful mission: to make international services accessible, transparent, and stress-free for everyone. What began as a small consultancy has grown into a full-service global agency trusted by thousands of individuals and businesses across 40+ countries.

Our founder, driven by personal experience navigating complex immigration and logistics challenges, built Alshendawi to be the partner he never had — one that combines deep expertise with genuine care for every client's outcome.

Today, our team of certified specialists spans immigration law, international logistics, language services, and business consulting — united by a shared commitment to getting results.
